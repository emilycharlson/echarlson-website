About

I’m Emily, a pianist, music-educator, and newly-converted software developer. Before 2020, my professional experience was heavily steeped in music, as a classical pianist and music educator. My transition to tech began at the onset of the pandemic when I was laid off from most of my work. With so much time on my hands, I started experimenting with HTML and CSS, and found I really enjoyed the puzzle and problem-solving. While 2020 was a dark and difficult year, it also brought new joy and opportunity. Not only did my partner and I welcome our first son, but I also chose to make a career pivot into tech by way of a 14-week coding bootcamp with Tech Elevator. I love holding down the fort with Java on the back-end, but also enjoy seeing the fruits of my labor on the front-end with Vue.js. When I am not learning new skills as a developer or in front of the piano, you can find me in nature, hiking with my family and two dogs.
